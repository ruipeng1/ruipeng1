FAQ for TRUE Debit PayPass Mastercard

  • What is TRUE Debit PayPass Mastercard?
    TRUE Debit PayPass Mastercard is a new debit card that allows you to perform
    payment through you NFC supported mobile handset.

  • What do I need in order to apply for the TRUE Debit PayPass Mastercard?
    For M1 mobile subscribers, you will need to subscribe to M1's NFC service and
    replace your current SIM card to an NFC SIM card at any M1 retail shop.

    For SingTel mobile subscribers, you will need to subscribe to Singtel's NFC
    service and replace your current SIM card to an NFC SIM card at any SingTel
    retail shop.

    For Starhub mobile subscribers, you will need to subscribe to Starhub's NFC
    service and replace your current SIM card to an NFC SIM card at any Starhub
    retail shop.

    You will also need to have an NFC supported mobile handset.

  • Am i eligible for debit card?
    You must be at least 15 years of age. You just need a Savings, Current or
    Hybrid a/c.

    If you are a foreigner, please apply for a debit card at any of our branches. You
    will need to present your passport and an employment pass that is valid for at
    least 6 months.

  • How to apply?
    You can apply through our online application form.

    If you require assistance with the card application, please contact the TRUE
    hotline 1800 123 3456 for assistance.
