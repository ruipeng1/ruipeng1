Overview

TRUE Debit PayPass Mastercard is a debit card that allow customers to perform payment via their NFC supported mobile handset. This debit card is a contactless payment method that gives our customers the convenience of making payment without handing over their payment card. Before you are able to use this mobile debit card, you must first download the smart wallet app from your mobile play store.

Features

  • Debit Card on mobile phone
  • Payment via your NFC supported mobile phone
  • Contactless payment method
  • Change your spending limit easily
  • Redeem mobile coupons on the go

Benefits

  • Enjoy hassle-free "Tap & Go" payment method
  • No more plastic, paper or wallet clutter
  • Enhanced Security
    • Lock your Smart Wallet Application with 6-digits pin number
    • Each transaction made will be recorded in your Smart Wallet App
