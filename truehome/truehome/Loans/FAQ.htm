How to pay back the loans?
You can start paying the loan back in monthly installments when you are back from overseas.
How will i know what is my monthly repayment amount and the due date?
Both the monthly repayment amount and date in which the bank will debit the repayment amount
from your designated bank account will be stated in the letter upon the approval of loan application.
Will i get a monthly statement detailing the payment and outstanding balance?
No monthly statements will be sent to you as monthly repayment amount is fixed every month. To find
out the outstanding balance of your loan account, please call our 24-hour customer service hotline at
1800 121 1234 or email us.
What documents are required for application of loan?
  1. Copy of NIRC / Passport
  2. Copy of empolyment pass / work permit (for foreigners)
  3. Income document
    • Pay slips from the last 6 months.
    • CPF contributes history of last 12 months.
    • 2 years' income tax statement for self-empolyment.
What should I expect after I submit my loan application? How quickly will my loan be applied?
Once you submit the application, the loan underwriting process begins. We will send you a conditional approval letter. This letter outline the steps we must take to complete the process as well as additional documentation that we need from you.
Can I change my application after I've submitted it?
Yes, you can make changes at any time before the bank loan amount sent to your account.
What is the eligibility to apply for the loan?
  • Age 21 & above
  • Annual income of S$2500 & above
  • Singaporean, Singapore PRs and foreigners
