The Idea of Travelling Loan

Travellers need to apply for this scheme before travelling overseas.
    (Click HERE to apply)
As our bank is linked with many banks in overseas, people are to take up     loans when they are in other countries through the internet.
We will locate your location through your mobile phones and communicate with the     overseas bank to notify the loan transactions.
A message will be sent to notify the collection of your money.
The transaction will be done within 10mins. It is a 24/7 service provided to the     people who need the money urgently.
You can obtain your money through the ATMs and the nearest bank by using the     credit card, typing in the particulars of your TRUE bank account and the security pin     number.
