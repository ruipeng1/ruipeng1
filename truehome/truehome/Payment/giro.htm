GIRO

Why do we need this service?

When students go to study overseas, their parents could be worried about their children waste money. And thus many of them prefer to transfer money monthly to their children’s account.

How do we apply this service?

The parents could connect their account with their children, our bank will transfer the fixed amount of money from the parents to the children’s account for them monthly and there will be given a discount of fee for transfer money between the connected accounts.
