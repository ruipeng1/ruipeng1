(1). First，the parents could connect their account with their children

(2). Then, our bank will transfer the fixed amount of money from the parents to the children’s account for them regularly and there will be given a discount of fee for transfer money between the connected accounts.

(3). The money in parent’s account will be treated as fixed deposit with different period with different interest rate.

(4). The users could state a minimum amount of the students’ card, if the money in their children’s accounts met that amount , a message will also send to the parents.
At the same time, an amount of money (the parents agree before) will transfer to their children automatically by a flash transfer service.

(5). An extra service will be provided to the connected accounts, if both of their accounts are in our bank. Every time after the parents transfer the money to their children’s account or their children withdraw money from their account, the parents could receive a message from our bank to tell them the remind money in their children’s account. When there is an emergency happened to the students. The money can be transfer at the fastest speed. They can bind their phone with their account and then we will provide them a pin number.
