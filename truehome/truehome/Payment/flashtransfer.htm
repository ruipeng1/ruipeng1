Flash Transfer

How it work

Flash transfer service will also be provided by our bank when there is an emergency happened to the students. The money can be transfer at the fastest speed. It can also work together with Message Warning Service.


You need to know

The amount of money will be $500-$50000; the rate of fee depends on: same bank, different bank, the amount of money. $50 is the highest money we charged.
