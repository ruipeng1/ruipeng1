Convenient Application for Promotion

How to know when there is an promotion going on for Fixed Deposit Account?

Existing customers will receive a message from us to let you know about the promotions details.
New customers will have to create a new bank account with us. They can receive the promotions details through a message from us.


How to apply for it?

If the customer receive the message regarding the promotion, they can reply the message with their name and the pin numbers provided in the message or their name with the other bank accounts details like account number (if they are not using their Fixed Deposit Account to apply for the promotion) or applies via internet banking.


What if our bank accounts do not meet the minimum requirement for the promotion?

If the customers’ Fixed Deposit Account/ or other accounts (the accounts that is used to apply for the promotion) does not meet the minimum requirement for the promotion, we will sent another message to them to inform them about the requirement that they did not met and the things they required to apply for the promotion.
Once the bank has confirmed the application for the promotion has been successfully applied, we will send a message to the customers to inform them about the successful application for the promotion.


Can we use our Fixed Deposit Account which is not within the maturity date to apply for the promotion?

No. If the promotion application period is not within the customers’ Fixed Deposit Account maturity date, customer will be asked to provide details of their other bank account that they had to use to apply for the promotion. For example, their Savings Account or other Fixed Deposit accounts that they hold with our bank or other banks.


If we do not want to apply for the promotion?

They do not need to do anything if they do not want to apply for the promotions.
