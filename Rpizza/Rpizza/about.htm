ABOUT US



The name of my company is Rpizza. Rpizza which had its humble beginning as fresh ingredient incorporated in Dec 2012. Carrying a spirit of creativity and customised the pizza for our customer. Rpizza has rapidly expanded to become a distinctive brand. With a global staff strength of 150 employees, Rpizza have 21 outlets in Singapore. Our company understand the needs of today’s busy families & individuals. Whether you are working late at the office or in-between dropping kids off at school or sports practice, you should always have a choice of a variety of fresh healthy & authentic dishes delivered right to your home or office. We offer the highest quality traditional & gourmet pizza, a wide variety of side orders, chicken wings, garlic bread and beverages. We pride ourselves for using 100% ingredient with no artificial flavouring added and up to 30% less cholesterol compared to regular pizza. Rpizza mission to strive customer satisfaction and proved excellent services.
